About Friends of People Trust

Friends of People Trust is a dedicated nonprofit organization committed to empowering underserved communities across India. we have worked tirelessly to improve access to education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods for rural and urban marginalized populations.

Our core mission is to create lasting social impact by collaborating with local communities, volunteers, and partners to foster growth, resilience, and self-reliance.

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their background, has equal opportunities to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain transparency and honesty in all our actions.
  • Empowerment: Enabling people to take charge of their own futures.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with communities and stakeholders for collective success.
  • Innovation: Embracing creative solutions to tackle social challenges.
